Empowering Our Youth: Our Mission and Vision

The Isiolo Youth Innovation Centre empowers local youths by offering education, resources, support, and community engagement. Through workshops, mentorship, and networking, we enable youths to develop skills, pursue opportunities, and drive positive change in Isiolo and beyond.
